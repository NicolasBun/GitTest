\subsection{Detailed Description}
\hyperlink{classembree_1_1_velvety}{Velvety} \hyperlink{classembree_1_1_b_r_d_f}{BRDF}. For materials with horizon scattering (velvet, peach, etc.) The \hyperlink{classembree_1_1_b_r_d_f}{BRDF} has a reflectance parameter that determines the color of the surface and a parameter to determine the falloff of horizon scattering.
