\subsection{Detailed Description}
\hyperlink{classembree_1_1_lambertian}{Lambertian} \hyperlink{classembree_1_1_b_r_d_f}{BRDF}. A lambertian surface is a surface that reflects the same intensity independent of the viewing direction. The \hyperlink{classembree_1_1_b_r_d_f}{BRDF} has a reflectivity parameter that determines the color of the surface.
