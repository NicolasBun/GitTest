\subsection{Detailed Description}
\hyperlink{classembree_1_1_minnaert}{Minnaert} \hyperlink{classembree_1_1_b_r_d_f}{BRDF}. For backscattering materials (e.g. dust, fabric) The \hyperlink{classembree_1_1_b_r_d_f}{BRDF} has a reflectance parameter that determines the color of the surface and a parameter to determine the amount of backscattering.
