\subsection{Detailed Description}
\hyperlink{classembree_1_1_specular}{Specular} Phong \hyperlink{classembree_1_1_b_r_d_f}{BRDF}. The cosine of the angle between incoming ray direction and reflection direction is raised to some power to approximate a glossy reflection.
