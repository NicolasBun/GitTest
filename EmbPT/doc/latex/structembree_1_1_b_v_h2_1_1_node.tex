\subsection{Detailed Description}
\subsubsection*{template$<$typename T$>$ struct embree::BVH2$<$ T $>$::Node}

\hyperlink{classembree_1_1_b_v_h2}{BVH2} \hyperlink{structembree_1_1_b_v_h2_1_1_node}{Node}. The nodes store for each dimension the lower and upper bounds of the left and right child in one SSE vector. This allows for a fast swap of the left and right bounds inside traversal for rays going from \char`\"{}right to left\char`\"{} in some dimension.
