\subsection{Detailed Description}
Implements a plastic material. A dielectric layer over a diffuse surface is modeled through a dieletric layer \hyperlink{classembree_1_1_b_r_d_f}{BRDF} for the covered diffuse part, and a microfacet \hyperlink{classembree_1_1_b_r_d_f}{BRDF} for the rough dielectric surface.
