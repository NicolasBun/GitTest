\subsection{Detailed Description}
Black Matter \hyperlink{classembree_1_1_b_r_d_f}{BRDF}. A black matter surface is a surface that reflects nothing, converting all received energy to heat. We use this \hyperlink{classembree_1_1_b_r_d_f}{BRDF} for a simple flat shading since we return the \char`\"{}color\char`\"{} of the surface. \begin{DoxyWarning}{Warning}
This is not physically possible unless the color is black 
\end{DoxyWarning}
