Detailed Description

template<typename Ground>
class embree::DielectricLayer< Ground >

Dielectric layer BRDF model.

Models a ground BRDF covered by a dielectric layer. The model works by refracting the incoming and outgoing light direction into the medium and then evaluating the ground BRDF. The model also supports a transmission coefficient of the dielectricum.
