Detailed Description

BRDF interface definition.

A BRDF can be evaluated, sampled, and the sampling PDF be evaluated. Note that in difference to the definition in the literature, our BRDFs contain the cosine term. E.g. the diffuse BRDF in our system is a/pi*cos(wi,N) instead of a/pi. This makes the formula of reflection and refraction BRDFs more natural. It further creates consistency as the sampling functionality of the BRDF class would have to include sampling of the cosine term anyway. As an optimization, the evaluation of the cosine term can even be skipped in case it is handled through sampling.
